Island of Hawai'i

What does the Big Island offer?

The Island of Hawai'i, or more commonly known as the Big Island, offers the most unique of landscapes to explore. From the humid jungles of Hilo, to the hot lavascape of Volcano National Park, to the snow capped top of Mauna Kea. This is one of the most unique places in the world because it has all but one of the world's weatherscapes (arctic).
