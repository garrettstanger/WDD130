Overview

Purpose

When anyone thinks of an ideal vacation what is always the chosen destination? It is always the beautiful Hawaiian Islands. But how are you supposed to know what to do when you go there? There are so many beautiful beaches to visit, but if that’s the only thing that you plan for, you are truly missing out on the true Hawaiian experience. My website is going to be your introduction to the only tour guide you will need to have the best experience possible when you visit these stunning islands.

Audience

The intended audience for this website is anyone looking to have the best experience possible when they decide to visit the Hawaiian Islands. Most of the travel companies will take you to the generic tourism spots where almost every tourist goes or has already seen before. Why should you have to take a tour bus with 50+ other tourists to see and visit the same sites as the thousands of others already do? Tour Hawaii is run by locals and will deliver a pricelessly unique experience that others don’t know how to offer.

Content

Home page

Hawai’i is one of the most beautiful vacation destinations in the entire world that anyone can experience. With stunning beaches almost around every corner, it is hard to find any fault with these isolated paradises.

But the beaches are just one of the magnificent qualities of this ultimate tropical escape! You have but to realize that Hawai’i offers a vast array of locations to visit and make into wonderful memories.

Images for the Home page

Island of Hawai'i

The Island of Hawai'i, or more commonly known as the Big Island, offers the most unique of landscapes to explore. From the humid jungles of Hilo, to the hot lavascape of Volcano National Park, to the snow capped top of Mauna Kea. This is one of the most unique places in the world because it has all but one of the world's weatherscapes (arctic).

Images for the Page 2

Kaua'i

Kaua'i is known as the Garden Island and for good reason. When someone daydreams about Hawai'i, Kaua'i is what they picture in their head. Called the most beautiful of the islands but the vacation veterans, it is hard to argue after you spend one day witnesses the beauty here. Kauai boasts some of the most scenic hikes in the world. The two most picturesque being the Na Pali Coast and Waimea Canyon, (you may have thought it the Grand Canyon at first glance!)
