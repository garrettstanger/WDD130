Overview

Purpose

When anyone thinks of an ideal vacation what is always the chosen destination? It is always the beautiful Hawaiian Islands. But how are you supposed to know what to do when you go there? There are so many beautiful beaches to visit, but if that’s the only thing that you plan for, you are truly missing out on the true Hawaiian experience. My website is going to be your introduction to the only tour guide you will need to have the best experience possible when you visit these stunning islands.

Audience

The intended audience for this website is anyone looking to have the best experience possible when they decide to visit the Hawaiian Islands. Most of the travel companies will take you to the generic tourism spots where almost every tourist goes or has already seen before. Why should you have to take a tour bus with 50+ other tourists to see and visit the same sites as the thousands of others already do? Tour Hawaii is run by locals and will deliver a pricelessly unique experience that others don’t know how to offer.
